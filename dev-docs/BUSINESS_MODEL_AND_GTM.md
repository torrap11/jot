# Business Model and Go-to-Market

> **Source**: Organized from `deep-research-report (1).md`

---

## Pricing Strategy

| Tier | Price | Features |
|------|-------|----------|
| **Free** | $0 | Limited captures, limited trigger types; habit formation |
| **Pro** | $10–$15/user/month | Unlimited capture, core triggers (apps/domains/calendar), AI interaction, encrypted sync |
| **Team** | $18–$25/user/month | Shared contexts, intent templates, admin controls |
| **Enterprise** | Custom | SSO, compliance, policy-driven data handling |

---

## Paths to Revenue

| Milestone | Route | ARR |
|-----------|-------|-----|
| **$1M** | 7,000 Pro users @ $12/mo, or 300 teams × 15 seats @ $20, or hybrid | ~$1M |
| **$10M** | 2,000 teams × 25 seats @ $20/mo (B2B2C) | ~$12M |
| **$50M** | 10,000 teams × 20 seats @ $20/mo; workflow intelligence layer | ~$48M |

---

## Go-to-Market: Initial Wedge

**Desktop power users** who live in keyboard-first tools and suffer from context switching: engineers, product leaders, founders, sales operators.

**MVP priorities:**
- Global hotkey capture (desktop)
- Browser domain triggers
- Calendar meeting triggers
- Contact/people linking
- Fast dismiss/snooze/done UX

**Distribution:**
- Keyboard-first communities, launcher ecosystems
- Developer ecosystems, open API, trigger packs
- Content marketing, demos
- Viral workflows, shareable intent templates

## Three-Year Execution Plan

| Year | Focus | Targets |
|------|-------|---------|
| **Year 1** | MVP, retention, narrow-context excellence | macOS + browser extension; 10k users; $150k–$400k ARR |
| **Year 2** | Scaling, deeper integrations, team monetization | 100k–250k users; $2M–$6M ARR |
| **Year 3** | Category leadership, platform, acquisition readiness | 500k–1.5M users; $10M+ ARR |

### Year 1 Details
- Ship macOS desktop + browser extension; Windows beta if feasible
- Core triggers: app open, domain open, calendar meeting start
- AI: task extraction, summaries, "ask my notes" Q&A
- Privacy: local-first, explicit consent, "why this fired"

### Year 2 Details
- More calendar providers, richer browser triggers, comms/work apps
- Context graph: propose triggers from captured text
- Team plan: shared intent templates, meeting-context prompts

### Year 3 Details
- Mature matching engine: suppression, personalization, explainability
- Android companion, iOS capture/notification companion
- Enterprise: SSO, policy controls, security reviews
